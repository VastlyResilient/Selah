Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Selah ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you subscribe to our daily Bible verse SMS service.

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to Selah, we collect the following information:

  • First and last name — to personalize your messages
  • Mobile phone number — to deliver your daily Bible verse via SMS
  • Preferred delivery time — to send messages at your chosen hour
  • Theme preference — (Encouragement, Wisdom, Peace, Strength, Faith, or Love) to curate relevant scripture

We do not collect payment information, passwords, or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Send you one daily Bible verse SMS at your chosen delivery time
  • Process unsubscribe requests when you reply STOP
  • Improve the relevance of scripture based on your selected theme

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. SMS Messaging

By subscribing, you consent to receive recurring automated SMS messages from Selah. Message frequency is once per day. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. To re-subscribe, visit our website. For help, reply HELP to any message.

4. Data Storage

Your subscriber information is stored securely on our servers. We retain your data only as long as you remain an active subscriber. When you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our active sending list.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Twilio may process your phone number in order to transmit messages on our behalf. Twilio's privacy policy can be found at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Selah is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: BOBBYATF@gmail.com
